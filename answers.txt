
Q1: Why might we consider using git?

Answer: Git serves as a version control software that can allow you to version your software and create branches of your work, allowing for multiple people to work on something at the same time and merge their work. It's nice because it is compatible with pretty much any and every IDE and operating system, so you can use it in windows powershell, the mac terminal and every popular IDE

Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines the histories of the two branches into one branch, rebasing puts changes from one branch at the beginning of another, ultimatelty rewriting the history
